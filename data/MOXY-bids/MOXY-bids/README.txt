Participants performed concentric and eccentric contractions on a isokinetic dynanometer at two different intensity (30%, and 50% of MVC).
Before the task, subjects were exposed to five minutes occlusion of the quadriceps.
Each participant performed two sessions of concentric or eccentric in a randomised order.
Each participant hab a portalite musculare NIRS system on the vastus medialis and semaxone muscular nirs system on the vastus lateralis.
